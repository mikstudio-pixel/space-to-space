SPACE TO SPACE

An international design project presenting works from twelve universities across nine countries. It connects physical and mental spaces, reality and fiction, science and art, exploring new ways of designing for a changing world – expanding into space while rethinking our relationship with Earth. The exhibition spans disciplines, from product and interior design to multimedia and material experiments. It views space as both physical and contextual, addressing environmental and social challenges. It represents a shift in thinking – design as a humane, ecological, and innovative response to systemic change.
